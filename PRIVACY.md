# Privacy Policy for Legit

**Last Updated:** [הכניסו תאריך]

The **Legit** extension is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user data.

## Local Storage
The extension uses your browser's **Local Storage** to save your preferences and settings. This data:
* Stays locally on your device.
* Is never sent to external servers.
* Is not accessible by the developers.
